Product features

Mixmi is a clean, powerful e-commerce platform built for creators and buyers alike. Sell your stickers without worrying about printing — buyers choose where and how to print them. And here's the twist: buyers can mix, match, and stack any stickers they want to create their own unique combinations.

Effortless Selling

Upload your stickers and start selling right away. No need to handle printing or fulfillment — Mixmi takes care of the setup so you can focus on creating.

Buyer-Controlled Printing

Buyers choose where and how their stickers get printed. You don't worry about logistics; they get full flexibility.

Creative Sticker Stacking

Unlike traditional marketplaces, Mixmi lets buyers mix, match, and stack multiple stickers into one custom design. Every purchase becomes a personal creation.

Simple, Powerful Platform

Built for ease and performance, Mixmi combines a smooth shopping experience with tools that empower both artists and buyers.
